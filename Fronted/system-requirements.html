System Requirements

For optimal performance, ensure your device meets the following specifications:

  • Supported Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari (latest versions)
  • Operating Systems: Windows 10+, macOS 10.13+, iOS 13+, Android 9+
  • Internet Speed: ≥ 5 Mbps download speed
  • Minimum Hardware: 4 GB RAM, microphone for interactive features

Keeping your OS and browser up to date ensures reliability and a responsive interface.
