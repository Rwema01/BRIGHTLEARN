Setting Up Your Profile

Customize your BRIGHTLEARN experience:

  1. 1 Click the profile icon in the top-right corner.
  2. 2 Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  3. 3 Fill in your name, add a photo, and write a short bio.
  4. 4 Click "Save" to update your profile.

A complete profile helps with personalization and notifications.
