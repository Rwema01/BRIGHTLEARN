Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you're facing any challenges, try the following solutions:

  • Pages won’t load: Clear browser cache and refresh.
  • Quizzes or lessons not responding: Ensure JavaScript is enabled.
  • Progress or scores not updating: Try logging out and back in or switch browsers.
  • No notifications: Make sure notifications are enabled in your profile settings.

If problems continue, reach out via the Help Center contact options.
